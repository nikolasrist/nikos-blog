Talks

My first main hobby when I moved to Bonn in 2016 was attending all kinds of meetups related to software development. Meetups are a great tool for networking and learning. My employers have always encouraged me to host meetups in their office, as they've proven to be a helpful recruiting measure.

Soon after I've started giving talks based on my work experience, I eventually created my own local Meetup group Bonn Code, where all talks must be in english, in order to provide a space for international attendees and speakers.
